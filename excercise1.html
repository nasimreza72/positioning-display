William Gibson

From Cyberspace to Cyberpunk

"The future is already here - it's just not evenly distributed."

  1. 1948 - born in South Carolina, USA
  2. 1967 - moved to Canada to avoid conscription to the war in Vietnam
  3. 1981 - his science fiction stories began appearing in magazines
  4. 1984 - Gibson's first novel Neuromancer is published, immediately becoming a classic of cyberpunk literature
  5. 1993 - 1999 - The Bridge Trilogy is released. It is comprised of three novels: Virtual Light, Idoru and All Tomorrow's Parties
  6. 2003 - 2010 - the novels Pattern Recognition, Spook Country and Zero History are released, comprising the Blue Ant Trilogy
  7. 2020 - his most recent novel Agency is published

We have no future because our present is too volatile. We have only risk management. The spinning of the given moment's scenarios. Pattern recognition.

- William Gibson
